Open Access Green Power (Renewable Power Trading)

Open Access lets large consumers (typically ≥ 1 MW) purchase renewable energy directly from solar/wind generators through the state grid—via Group Captive or Third-Party Sale.

Contact us for a free feasibility study

Key Benefits

  • 20–40% savings on electricity bills.
  • Fixed tariff for 10–15 years.
  • Zero upfront investment (third-party model).
  • Helps meet RPOs and sustainability goals.

How It Works

  1. Power Purchase — Sign PPA (Group Captive or Third-Party).
  2. Wheeling — Energy injected into grid and wheeled to your HT service.
  3. Billing — OA landed cost + statutory charges; grid as backup.

Solutions We Offer

Group Captive

Equity participation for tariff certainty; qualifies as captive.

Third-Party Sale

No equity; pay-as-you-consume under long-term PPA.

Bundled Rooftop + OA

Combine on-site rooftop with OA to maximise savings.

Advisory & Compliance

SLDC, CEIG, metering, banking, and RPO documentation.
